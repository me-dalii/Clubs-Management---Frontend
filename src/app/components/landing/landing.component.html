Faculty of Sciences of Bizerte

The Faculty of Science of Bizerte is a teaching and research institution under the supervision of the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research. It is attached to the University of Carthage. The Faculty of Sciences of Bizerte was founded in 1990 (Law No. 106 of 26 November 1990) and replaced the Ecole Normale Supérieure created in 1956 in Tunis and transferred to Bizerte in October 1980.

4C

The Career and Skills Certification Center (4C) of the University of Tunis is a structure attached to the presidency of the University. The 4C of the University of Carthage plays the role of a link between the university, the student and the company. Its mission is to prepare and support students and graduates and to facilitate their integration into the labor market.
